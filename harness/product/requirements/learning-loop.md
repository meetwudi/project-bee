# Requirement: Learning Loop

## Summary

The product must help a learner maintain a learning loop based on positive reinforcement.

## Source

Human product direction, 2026-06-10 and 2026-06-11.

## Loop

The product learns what the learner likes to learn and how the learner learns. The learner can give digestible feedback so the product better understands their preferences.

The loop should account for both the learner's existing narrow view of the world and intentional exploration outside the learner's usual zone.

## Wording Gaps

- The exact term for intentional exploration outside the learner's usual zone is not settled.
