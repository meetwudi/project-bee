# Requirement: Learning Loop

## Summary

The product must help a learner maintain an adaptive loop across learning inputs, learner interests, next-step discovery, and sensemaking.

## Source

Human product direction, 2026-06-10.

## Acceptance Tests

- Given the learner has added or connected learning information, the product can show what the learner appears to be learning.
- Given the learner has a history of topics or materials, the product can suggest what to learn next.
- Given many learning inputs, the product can summarize or organize them into a coherent view.
- Given new learner activity over time, the product can update its understanding of the learner's tendencies.

## Open Questions

- What input types count as learning information?
- What signals define "what I tend to learn"?
- Should next-step discovery be manually approved, agent-driven, or both?
- What evidence should the product show when it makes a recommendation?
